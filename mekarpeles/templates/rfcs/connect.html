Problem with the web - information is changing. A book is static point, you can reference a point. But the webpage is a line of points, and there is no way to accommodate this. When you view a page as an repeat observer, all you can discern is the slope of this line, given the points you have physically archived or cached in your mind. Other than by personal accountability, there is no mechanism for retroactively addressing or retrieving a previous point.
